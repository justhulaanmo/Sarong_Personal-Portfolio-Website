Restaurant Management System

Features & Screenshots


Login – Secure login for admin and customers.

Admin Home – Central hub for management.


Dashboard Menu – Quick access to features.

Admin Add to Cart – Admin can restock products.

Forgot Password – Recovery option for users.


Customer’s Profile – Manage personal details.

View Cart – Shows items ordered by customer.


Sales Report – Admin can track sales records.

Manage Products – Edit prices, names, and stock.


Customer Orders – Track total purchases per customer.
