About Me

I am a 3rd-year BSIT student at BISU Balilihan whose path has been shaped by diverse work experiences and continuous growth. Before returning to my studies, I worked in various roles—including personal shopper, call center agent for Meralco and PLDT, and staff in our family’s pack-launch business. Balancing day work and night training taught me resilience, discipline, and adaptability.

Since continuing my education in Bohol, I have served as an SSG officer for two years, joined the Peer Facilitators Circle, and participated in multiple campus activities that strengthened my leadership and teamwork skills.

My journey has not been linear, but every experience has guided me toward one clear realization: I am passionate about the world of Information Technology, and I am committed to building a future in this field.
